How does it work?

When sharing is set to Public, the share link for this profile is enabled. This share link is a separate webpage that displays a summary of the drugs, conditions, and allergies for this particular profile.


How can I send the share link?

  • Copy and paste the share link into an email or txt message.
  • Visit the page and tap the share button on your device to view sharing options.
  • Verbally communicate the share link to someone over the phone or in person.


Who can access the page?

The page is publicly accessible with no sign-in required. Anyone with the share link can view the page.


Your privacy

To protect your privacy, the share link does not contain the personally identifiable information of your profile (your profile name, email, etc).Sharing can be disabled at any time. The share link will automatically disable if your account is inactive for more than 1 year.


What about my other profiles?

Sharing status is unique to every profile in your account. Changing the sharing status for one profile will not affect the sharing status of your other profiles.
